Wave function collapse — interpretations
PhysicsDraftmx_robin · Updated 2h ago
Wave function collapse refers to the phenomenon where a quantum system transitions from a superposition of states to a single definite state upon measurement. Different interpretations of quantum mechanics provide distinct explanations for this phenomenon, including the Copenhagen interpretation, many-worlds, and pilot wave theory.
