Every section in the left sidebar is fully clickable and navigates between the 9 views. Here's what each section covers:

The overview is the home screen — four metric cards (MAU, articles, donations, moderation queue), a 30-day traffic sparkline with session stats, a live moderation queue with approve/reject actions, a top articles table, a real-time activity feed, and a subject coverage bar chart.

Analytics has the full monthly bar chart for 2025, traffic source breakdowns with progress bars, top referring domains, and a detailed engagement table with tree interaction counts per article.

Content pipeline is a Kanban board — Draft, Under Review, Approved, and Published columns, each with article cards showing author, subject badge, and status. Items in review have an amber left border; approved items have green.

Moderation queue shows the full 12-item queue with article diffs (red strikethrough / green addition), resource additions, new article submissions, and spam reports, each with contextual action buttons that differ by item type.

Users & roles has the full user list with role badges, an editable permissions matrix showing what each role level can do, and filter chips to segment by role.

Finance shows YTD totals, MRR, expense ratio, and runway, with a scrollable donation ledger (color-coded by type: monthly, one-time, grant), and a budget allocation breakdown.

Discussions is a sortable table of all open threads across the site, filterable by resource disputes, article talk, and governance — with reply counts, last activity, and status.

Resource library gives the org a management view of all 92,400 resources with dispute counts flagged in red, type badges, tree appearances, and edit buttons.

Settings has a two-column layout with a section nav (General, Appearance, Moderation rules, Notifications, etc.), organization detail fields, and toggle switches for all platform configuration — all interactive.
